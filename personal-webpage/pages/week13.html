Final Website

This interactive puzzle was aesthetically inspired by the exquisite works of artists from Renaissance era up to the 19th century. The simulation allows the user play with visuals and "enter into artist's mind" so-to speak. The movement of the mouse across the image grids feels relaxing and satistyfing, and at the same time invites the user to passively participlate in his imagination. [Considering the difference of the screen settings in each browser and computer, the content view may vary, so it's best to have 100% zoom to be able to fully engage with the objects]. Open Here.
